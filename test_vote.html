Ashley Chen

陈原娇



Yuanjiao Chen(Ashley)is from Zhoushan, Zhejiang Province.She

graduated from University of Auckland with BCom/BMus conjoint

degree and is currently doing an Accounting job. Previously she won

“Ten outstanding singer” award in “Zhoushan City Singing

Competition” and “Top six” in the “International Chinese New Talent

Singing Championship 2010 New Zealand Final”.Ashley is good at

lyric songs but she can manage various styles as well.She is able to

perform different types of songs based on their style.Now let’s look

forward to watching her performance!

陈原娇Ashley，来自浙江舟山。Ashley毕业于奥克兰大学商科和音乐双学士学位，目

前主要从事会计工作。她曾在舟山市举办的歌唱比赛中获“十大优秀歌手”奖，更是

2010年全球华人新秀歌唱大赛纽西兰区选拔赛六强！Ashley天生乐感很强，最擅长

的是抒情歌曲，却也能驾驭各种风格。聪慧的她可以根据各种歌曲的风格演绎不同

的唱法，这使她在海选时就得到评委的一致赞赏，不知道台上的她会给我们带来怎

样的惊喜!

Bridget Wang

王舒眉



Bridget studied Film, Television and Media Studies at the University

of Auckland and graduated last year. She took private singing

lessons for a few years. Singing is a passion and hobby of hers.

Surprisingly, this year Bridget performed in the Musgrove Theatre

stage as part of a musical! Musical is truly a platform to test the

performer’s singing ability and body coordination. Now we look

forward to appreciating her performance on 17th October!

王舒眉Bridget去年毕业于奥克兰大学电影及媒体系。从小学习拉小提琴的她很喜欢

唱歌，尤其对爵士和摇滚乐情有独钟。值得一提的是，Bridget曾在 Musgrove 歌剧院

参演过i-musical 歌舞剧！天了噜，歌舞剧可是很考验歌者实力的舞台，不仅要求表

演者的气息稳定，还对肢体协调能力有很高的要求！看来王舒眉的表演一定有很强

的感染力，真是迫不及待想看到她在决赛舞台上的表现了！

Terence Gong

龚浚霖



Team member Terence can be regarded as Youjia Lin in Auckland!

He is from Hong Kong. As a Cantonese speaker, Terrence is quite

fond of Cantonese songs. If you wanna find out how good his

singing is,come to our Final Night on 17th October and cheer for him!

龚浚霖Terence已经在新西兰生活了一年多。大家有木有觉得第一眼看他很像台湾歌手

林宥嘉！不过Terence并不是台湾人，而是来自香港。之前小编已经多次听到粉丝对他

的呼唤，那就快来决赛现场一观，你们将会听到他现场演绎的经典粤语歌曲，千万不

要错过哦~

Marco Xu

许少剑



Shaojian (Marco) Xu who graduated from AUT last year and now he

is an in house accountant. If you have participated in previous

DreamWorks singing contest, you may be familiar with him. He had

joined DreamWorks in both 2011 and 2012. This year is the third

time he stands on DreamWorks’ stage. Because of his persistence,

we are able to listen to his beautiful voice and his excellent

performance. Now, wish his persistence would help him win the

reward , wish his dream could come true on the DreamWorks stage!

Fingers cross!

许少剑Marco，去年从AUT毕业的他是名公司内部会计。如果有朋友关注新秀梦工厂肯

定会觉得他有点眼熟。参加过2011、2012的新秀梦工厂以及多个月歌唱比赛，身为

2013年华人新秀亚军的他可谓是歌唱比赛界资深参赛者了！正因为有他对音乐的狂热

喜爱，我们才有机会听到如此让人如痴如醉的歌声。坚持不懈的他相信“天道酬勤”

，希望他的坚持不懈以及稳扎稳打实力超人的唱功和动人的歌声能为他带来他想要的

，愿他梦想成真！尽请期待这位唱功高超的实力唱将在决赛当晚给我们带来如何震撼

人心的表演吧！

2015 Barfoot & Thompson Greenlane DreamWorks Singing Contest

Finals Night!

濮睿璇Sherry现在就读于梅西大学商科系，主修会计的她从小热爱音乐喜欢唱歌。虽

然不是音乐专业但是因为从小就发自内心的爱唱歌，Sherry经常在自己有空的时候学

习音乐知识。也是因为喜欢唱歌，今年她终于站上了新秀梦工厂的舞台！可爱迷人又

不失实力的她会在决赛当晚给我们带了怎样的歌声呢？是如清风般拂过心扉的轻松舒

畅，还是犹如一朵玫瑰的艳丽动人呢？尽请期待吧！

Victor Huang

黄达介



Dajie (Victor) Huang is from Dalian, Liao Ning Province. He is

currently working as a market specialist in New Zealand tourism

industry. Even though, Victor didn’t have experienced any

professional training nor attempted any singing contest before, his

good voice and awesome singing skill make him get into the Finals

Night directly! He is good at singing love songs, but what kind of

songs he will perform to us on the Finals Night? Let’s find out at

2015 Barfoot & Thompson Greenlane DreamWorks Singing Contest

Finals Night!

黄达介Victor，来自辽宁大连，目前从事新西兰旅游行业的市场专员工作。热爱唱歌

的他虽然没有进行过专业的声乐训练也没有参加过歌唱比赛，但声音好听而且实力高

超唱功可圈可点。擅长唱情歌的他会跟我们带来怎样精彩的表演呢？是会让人如入歌

境潸然泪下的感动人心，还是会让人像浸了蜜糖般的甜蜜欢喜呢？尽请期待自信的情

歌小王子Victor在10月17日新秀梦工厂决赛给我们带来感动人心的歌唱吧！

Max Xu

徐铭辰



Max Xu is our ‘newest’ member of this competition as he is a recent immigrant

who has only been in NZ for 4 months. His singing skills have been developed

throughout his university time. He has participated in choir and singing clubs

and was even placed in the top 100 in the 2011 Chinese ‘Sing Star Contest.’ Max

is an individual with an extremely unique voice and who is able to immerse and

connect with the songs he is singing straight away.

作为刚来到新西兰四个多月的“新人”，徐铭辰Max对绵羊国的一切充满了期待~

音乐一直是他最大的爱好，大学的经历更为他积累了很多表演经验。2011年他

曾参加 “蒙牛酸酸乳成就音乐梦想”并获得了中国西北赛区总冠军，成功晋级全

国100强。相信他的表现一定会给我们带来惊喜与感动！

Yan Xue

薛艳



YanXue, is an exceptionally creative architect who is studying a Masters degree

in Urban Design at University of Auckland. She believes that happiness is at the

core of her music and that music competitions are only another platform for

singers to express themselves. Having had a passion since she was a child, she

believes that music is in her blood and ancestry,and she was born to perform.

来自中国新疆克拉玛依的薛艳已经在新西兰生活一年多，现在在奥克兰大学攻

读城市设计硕士学位。广袤的家乡给了她开朗的性格。关于音乐，她会说：“通

常我用我的歌声自娱自乐，或许我不专业，但唱歌是我的爱好。”关于比赛，她

会说：“希望我的歌声可以愉悦听众，或许我不会赢，但娱乐是我的精神。”希

望你会微笑，在她的歌声里！

Yilin Li

李宜霖



Yilin is currently a student at University of Auckland studying accounting. She

holds a gifted voice and started singing at a young age and believes that beautiful

songs and lyrics can touch anyone and everyone regardless of their race, age or

gender. She loves country music and is impassioned about embracing

individuality whether it is in music or reality.

李宜霖目前在奥克兰大学就读会计专业。虽然没有接受过专业的声乐指导，但

她却有着惊人的歌唱天赋和对情绪d精准把握，能够令每个听她唱歌的人跟着音

乐的情绪或喜或悲。宜霖最喜欢民谣，海选时她的表现得到了评委们的一致好

评，让我们期待她在决赛现场的惊喜表现！

Claire Cai

蔡圣琳



Claire has a very recognisable voice. She has learnt piano since she was

four. Claire had many experiences with music at such a young age. If you

think she looks familiar, then you are not mistaken! She is the one of the 12

finalists from last year, made it into the finals for Voice of China NZ division,

and is also a powerful contestant from the TVB New Talent singing

Championship 2012. Claire is definitely a competitive contestant that is not to

be missed.

声音辨识度极高的蔡圣琳小姑娘Claire，其实4岁就习钢琴，品音乐啦。年纪轻

轻却经验十足，积累了无数大赛经验和舞台经验。如果这次你看到她感到眼熟

，没错！她就是去年新秀选手的12强!! 还是眼熟？没错！她就是去年中国好

声音新西兰区的决赛选手!!! 还是眼熟？没错！她就是2012TVB新秀决赛的种

子选手!!! 简直是实力无穷，法力无边的实力型兼美型选手！

Geoffrey Kong

孔仁豪



Geoffrey is currently studying at ACG International College. Even though he

didn’t participate in the Voice Of China, he was able to sing on stage in front

of the judges, and was complimented by the Chief Director of the program. He

says that he loves music, not only for the beautiful tune but also the

sympathetic feeling that he feels from his heart. His favourite genre is folk

music, because folk music is able to produce the deepest emotion by pouring

out heart and soul.

Geoffery是个ACG语言在读生。 这个大男孩，虽然没有参加好声音的比赛，但

机缘巧合之下他走上了好声音的舞台，只是试唱一下就被总导演夸奖。他说，

自己爱音乐，不仅享受优美的乐调，更是对听音乐时产生的共鸣的青睐。他喜

欢民谣也爱摇滚。“因为有人要呐喊，所以必须要有摇滚；因为有人要倾诉，

所以必须要有民谣。” 他认为：有时，静静地倾诉一首歌比摇旗呐喊一种爆

裂信仰更能直击人心。这是这个男孩的座右铭，也是他的信条。

Xuwei Luo

罗旭伟



Xuwei Luo is a current Commerce student studying at the University of

Auckland. He says he’s just a student who can sing one or two songs for fun,

but is diligent in what he is doing! For him, music is not a serious burden, but

the most beautiful bonus to his daily life. He loves music. Every different

experience in life gives him different comprehensions to music. Xuwei’s voice,

is relaxed yet elegant.

目前奥克兰大学商科学士在读的高音小能手罗旭伟，坚持对音乐玩玩儿的心态

，认真的对待！对他而言，音乐不是沉重的负担，而是生活琐碎中最最美好，

最最崇高的加分项。他爱音乐，生活中所有的爱与美好，痛与感悟都带给他对

音乐的不同理解。痞雅，是最能描绘他非凡歌声的形容。

Who would you like to give your vote to?
